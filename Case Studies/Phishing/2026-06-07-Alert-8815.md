# Incident Report: Alert 8815 - Phishing Email
**Date:** 2026-06-07 | **Status:** Closed | **Classification:** True Positive

---

## 1. Incident Details
* **Time of Activity:** 2026-06-07 07:33:50
* **Sender:** `urgents@amazon.biz`
* **Recipient:** `h.harris@thetrydaily.thm`
* **Subject:** Your Amazon Package Couldn’t Be Delivered – Action Required
* **URL:** `hxxp://bit[.]ly/3sHkX3da12340`

---

## 2. Analysis & Escalation
* **True Positive Reason:** Uses a fake domain (`@amazon.biz` instead of `.com`), creates fake delivery urgency, and hides the destination with a `bit.ly` shortener.
* **Escalation Reason:** **Yes.** The email bypassed filters and successfully reached the user's inbox, creating a high risk of execution.

---

## 3. Attack Indicators
* **Domain Spoofing:** Deceptive external email address.
* **Social Engineering:** Fake delivery scare tactics.
* **Obfuscation:** Masked malicious link.

---

## 4. Remediation Actions
1. **Purge:** Delete the email from the user's mailbox.
2. **Block:** Add `amazon.biz` to the mail gateway blocklist.
3. **Train:** Enroll the employee in phishing awareness training.
