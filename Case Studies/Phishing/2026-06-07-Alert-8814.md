# Incident Report: Alert 8814 - Phishing Triage
**Date:** 2026-06-07 | **Status:** Resolved | **Classification:** False Positive

## 1. Description
Suspicious inbound email targeting `j.garcia@thetrydaily.thm`. The email contained a link to a third-party domain (`hrconnex.thm`) regarding "Finalizing Onboarding Profile."

## 2. Methodology
* **Tool:** Splunk SIEM
* **Filter Used:** `sender="onboarding@hrconnex.thm" recipient="j.garcia@thetrydaily.thm"`
* **Procedure:** 1. Filtered email logs to isolate communication between the vendor and the target.
    2. Verified the vendor domain `hrconnex.thm` against internal HR-sanctioned vendor lists.
    3. Correlated email timestamps with company onboarding schedules.
* **Findings:** Confirmed that `hrconnex.thm` is a legitimate, company-sanctioned third-party vendor. The email is authorized business traffic.
